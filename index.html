With turquoise blue waters, picturesque islands and a touch of colonization, Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a slice of paradise on earth, a must in every traveler’s bucket list. Waterways being the only source of travel between the islands in the Andaman archipelago, a reliable ferry service is the most essential need for any traveler and there cannot be anyone better than Makruzz when it comes to choosing the right travel partner in the Islands.
